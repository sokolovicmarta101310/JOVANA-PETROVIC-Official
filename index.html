Jovana Petrović

Goalkeeper

Date of Birth: 11.09.2001.
Nationality: Serbia
Height: 174 cm
Weight: 63 kg
Position: Goalkeeper
Current Club: Red Star Belgrade (Serbia)

Player Profile

Dedicated, brave and passionate goalkeeper known for exceptional speed, sharp decision-making, and precise positioning. Strong in reading the game and managing depth, with excellent handling of crosses and confident footwork under pressure. Reliable in one-vs-one situations and highly effective in goal-line defense. Recognized for calmness in critical moments, leadership from the back line, direct communication and consistent performance at the highest level.

In addition to my playing career, I hold a UEFA B coaching license and am currently attending the UEFA B Goalkeeping course. I am fluent in Serbian, English, and Turkish which allows me to adapt easily to different environments and communicate effectively with teammates and coaching staff from various backgrounds.

Key Skills

  • Quick reactions and excellent game reading
  • Strong positioning and depth control
  • Confident handling of crosses and aerial balls
  • One-vs-one defending and goal-line saves
  • Precise ball distribution, play from behind and excellent footwork
  • Communication and organization of the defense
  • Composure and focus under pressure
  • Multilingual communication (Serbian, English, Turkish)

Mental Skills

The athlete demonstrates a high level of selective attention and concentration throughout the game. In critical situations, she reacts calmly and maintains focus through intensive mental training. In cases of personal mistakes, she shows exceptional emotional recovery speed and great emotional flexibility.

Her self-confidence is stable, positively influencing overall performance. She displays strong motivation and a competitive spirit, with a clear ability to take responsibility during key moments. She shows remarkable emotional resilience when facing both internal and external stress factors.

Her communication with the defensive line is clear and direct. She effectively manages her professional and personal resources, maintaining balance and optimal mental performance under pressure.
